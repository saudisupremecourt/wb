Notable dates in the Islamic year


-------------------------

1st of Muharram: the Islamic New Year.

10th of Muharram: Day of Ashura.

12th of Rabi al-Awwal: Birth of the Prophet.

27th of Rajab: Isra and Mi'raj.

15th of Sha'ban: Mid-Sha'ban/Forgiveness Night.

1st of Ramadan: The first day of Islamic fasting.

27th of Ramadan: Start of the Revelation of the Qur’an.

21st -29th of Ramadan: includes Laylat al-Qadr.

Last friday of Ramadan: Jumu'atul-Wida

1st of Shawwal: Eid ul-Fitr.

8th to 13th Dhu al-Hijjah: The Hajj pilgrimage to Mecca.

9th of Dhu al-Hijjah: Day of Arafa.

10th of Dhu al-Hijjah: Eid al-Adha.
